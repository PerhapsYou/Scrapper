Program Learning Outcomes
Upon graduation, the students should be able to:
PLO 1: Pursue continuing professional development in the field of architecture;
PLO 2: Communicate effectively in oral and written mediums both in English and Filipino;
PLO 3: Work effectively and independently in multi -disciplinary and multi -cultural teams;
PLO 4: Recognize professional, social and ethical responsibility;
