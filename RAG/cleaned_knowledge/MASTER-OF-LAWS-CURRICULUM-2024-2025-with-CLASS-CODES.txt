=== Section: 1. Basic Courses ===

=== Section ===
All LL.M. students are required to complete a total of six (6) units of basic
courses, as follows:
(a) Introduction to Canon Law (3 units)
This course is a survey of special topics selected from the 1983 Code of Canon
Law of the Catholic Church and its most important recent amendments. It traces the
history, sources and structure of the Code, with an overview of the meaning of
Ecclesiastical law, the people of God; hierarchical structures in the Church;
=== End ===

=== Section ===
consecrated and apostolic life; teaching, sanctifying and governing functions in the
Church; temporal goods; sanctions and processes in the Church.
