=======================================================
Bachelor of Science in Radiologic Technology (BSRT) PROGRAM EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES (PEOs)
=======================================================
Within 3–5 years after graduation, BSRT alumni are expected to:
1. Demonstrate empathy, sympathy, and compassion in patient care 
   and safe handling of imaging equipment.
2. Perform creative and effective radiographic procedures across 
   varying conditions and resources.
3. Exhibit expertise in medical imaging systems through continued 
   quality education.
4. Engage in community projects and research in Radiologic Technology.

=======================================================
Bachelor of Science in Radiologic Technology (BSRT) PROGRAM LEARNING OUTCOMES (PLOs)
=======================================================
Graduates of the BSRT program will be able to:
1. Apply interdisciplinary knowledge (sciences, humanities) in 
   radiologic practice.
2. Compare imaging and therapy modalities for proper application.
3. Demonstrate technical radiologic skills for accurate diagnosis 
   and compassionate care.
4. Communicate effectively in both English and Filipino.
5. Implement radiation safety, MRI protocols, and informed consent 
   procedures.
6. Maximize imaging machine life and departmental operations.
7. Participate in research and lifelong learning.
8. Practice the profession within legal, ethical, and moral standards.
9. Adapt technical skills to varied clinical scenarios.
10. Combine critical thinking and Christian values in leadership or 
    entrepreneurial settings.
11. Advocate patriotism and service to the Filipino people.
12. Live and serve according to CICM Christian values.

=======================================================
Bachelor of Science in Radiologic Technology (BSRT) ADMISSION POLICIES
=======================================================

UNDERGRADUATE FRESHMEN:
- Must pass the SLU College Entrance Examination (SLU-CEE).
- Admission starts mid-April; exams are conducted October to December.

TRANSFER STUDENTS:
- Must pass:
    - Qualifying Exam (QE)
    - Personality Test
    - Interview
- GWA requirement: 80% or higher.
- RadTech major/professional subjects from previous institutions 
  will not be credited.

GRADUATE PROGRAM STUDENTS:
- Master’s Degree:
    - Must have a Baccalaureate degree in a relevant field.
- Doctorate:
    - Must have a Master’s degree in a related field.

FOREIGN STUDENTS:
- Apply at least 6 months before the term begins.
- Arrive in Baguio 4 weeks before classes start for:
    - English Proficiency Test (EPT)
    - SLU-CEE / QE / GPEE
    - Personality Test
- Must undergo pre-admission processing and hold a valid Student Visa.
