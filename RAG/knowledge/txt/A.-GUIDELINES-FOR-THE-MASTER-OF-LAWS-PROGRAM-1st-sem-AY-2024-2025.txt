SCHOOL OF LAW – LL.M. PROGRAM
---------------------------------

1. What is the LL.M. Program of Saint Louis University?  
The LL.M. Program is a 48-unit, non-thesis postgraduate law degree delivered fully online.

2. When will the LL.M. Program open?  
The program will begin in August 2024.

3. Who is qualified to enroll?  
Graduates with an LL.B. or J.D. degree. Passing the Bar is NOT required.

4. Can enrollment be done online?  
Yes, but original paper documents must be submitted in person or by mail to:  
Office of the Dean, Room 414, Diego Silang Building, Saint Louis University.

5. When do classes and enrollment start?  
Enrollment begins at least a week before classes, which officially begin in August 2024.

6. What is the class schedule and modality?  
All classes are online. Classes meet once a month on one Saturday and one Sunday.  
First semester: August–December  
Second semester: January–June

PROGRAM DETAILS
---------------------------------

7. Duration:  
The LL.M. Program may be completed in two years. Students may take up to 12 units per semester.

8. Is a thesis required?  
No thesis. However, written papers and exams are required for each subject.

9. Total Units:  
48 units. Minimum enrollment per semester is 6 units, maximum is 12. Courses are fixed.

10. Professors:  
Taught by law professors and legal scholars from outside Baguio City.

11. Tuition Fees:  
- 12 units: Php 65,000.00  
- 9 units: Php 54,500.00  
- 6 units: Php 44,000.00  
Minimum payment upon enrollment: Php 23,000.00 (non-refundable)

13. Can I enroll if my documents aren't ready by August 2024?  
Yes, but you must execute a promissory note for submission of all documents within the first month.

14. How do I submit documents?  
a. Download forms via official links  
b. Save documents as PDF  
c. Rename using: `<LastName FirstName DocumentType>`  
   Example: DELA CRUZ JUANITA APPLICATION FORM  
d. Email all files with subject: `SUBMISSION DOCUMENTARY REQUIREMENTS`  
   Send to: sol.llm@slu.edu.ph

15. What happens after submission?  
The School of Law will assess your documents. If approved, you will be sent an Authorization to Enlist.

16. Who do I contact for queries?  
Email: sol.llm@slu.edu.ph
