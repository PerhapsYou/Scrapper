VISION & MISSION  
-----------------------------------------------------------  
The School of Advanced Studies (SAS) is envisioned as a Graduate School that stands for excellence and innovation,  
and that will be globally identified for the distinction of its programs and the quality of its research.  

SAS HISTORY  
-----------------------------------------------------------  
- SLU began its collegiate programs in 1952.  
- In 1956, the master's program in Education received government recognition.  
- Over the years, graduate programs expanded into various disciplines:  
  ‚Ä¢ Management Engineering (1956)  
  ‚Ä¢ English (1957)  
  ‚Ä¢ Business Management (1966)

- In 2018, SLU consolidated all graduate programs under the School of Advanced Studies (SAS).  
- SAS comprises 7 academic clusters: ABM, CIT, ENGG, LIBARTS, NATSCI, NRSG, TEACHED.  

===========================================================  
GOALS AND OBJECTIVES  
-----------------------------------------------------------  
1. Enhance intellect and creativity through excellent instruction, research, and extension work  
2. Promote highest ethical standards in scholarship and practice  
3. Hone professional and leadership skills for community and industry impact  
4. Offer community service opportunities through extension programs  
5. Produce internationally recognized research  
6. Employ expert faculty and invite international scholars  
7. Build academic-industry-government partnerships  
8. Elevate global visibility of the graduate school
