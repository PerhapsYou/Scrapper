BA COMMUNICATION – PROGRAM CATALOG

SLU VISION-MISSION  
Saint Louis University is envisioned as an excellent missionary and transformative educational institution zealous in developing human resources imbued with Christian Spirit and who are creative, competent, and socially involved.

SCHOOL VISION-MISSION  
The School of Teacher Education and Liberal Arts (STELA) is envisioned as an institution for excellence in Teacher and Liberal Arts Education, committed to the CICM mission of education of “missio et excellentia” in the formation and training of competent, creative, socially-involved professionals imbued with Christian Spirit.

PROGRAM EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES (PEO)  
Graduates of the BA Communication program are expected to:  
- Serve as professional media practitioners imbued with Christian Spirit, cognizant of the dignity of humanity and all creation.  
- Demonstrate relevant and responsive communication knowledge and skills that foster creativity, critical thinking, and scholarly research needed for national and international development.  
- Exhibit excellence in the theory and practice of communication applicable in various professions and contexts, with focus on the media professions.  
- Advocate the promotion of cultural heritage, social justice, nationalism, and community-based programs to improve quality of life.

PROGRAM LEARNING OUTCOMES (PLO)  
Graduates will be able to:  
- Apply communication theories, models, principles, practices, and tools in communicating through different media platforms and formats.  
- Exhibit mastery in using media equipment and in developing media plans and materials in various formats and platforms.  
- Construct effective spoken and written discourses in English or Filipino using Mass Communication registers.  
- Demonstrate skills and ethics in communication management, leadership, and entrepreneurship in media-related contexts.  
- Conduct development-oriented communication and media research and apply findings in practice.  
- Stay updated with the latest developments in the communication and media profession.  
- Participate in the generation, dissemination, and utilization of new knowledge and developmental projects.  
- Work effectively and independently in multidisciplinary and multicultural teams.  
- Uphold professional, social, and ethical standards in information handling and media practice.  
- Promote Filipino culture and values through mass communication.  
- Engage in lifelong learning and professional development.  
- Practice Christian values in personal and professional endeavors in service of the CICM mission.
