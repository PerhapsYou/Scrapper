SLU VISION-MISSION
=======================================================
Saint Louis University is envisioned as an excellent missionary and transformative educational institution zealous in developing human resources imbued with Christian Spirit and who are creative, competent and socially involved.

=======================================================
SAMCIS VISION-MISSION
=======================================================
The School of Accountancy, Management, Computing, and Information Studies (SAMCIS) is envisioned as the center of excellence in Business, Management, and Information Technology Education, committed to the creative, competent, and Christian formation of individuals, who can be agents of progress in the changing local and international business and information technology environments.

=======================================================
PROGRAM EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES (PEOs)
=======================================================
Within 3–5 years after graduation, BSTM graduates are expected to:

1. Adopt Christian practices and values to manifest compassion and ethical behavior in human relations and professional settings.
2. Continuously apply relevant and innovative knowledge and skills that promote creativity, sustainability, and cultural preservation in the tourism industry.
3. Demonstrate professionalism, competence, and collaboration in global and multicultural tourism environments.
4. Exhibit appreciation of host cultures and uphold the welfare of communities through responsible tourism.
5. Apply social science, tourism regulations, and management principles in real-world industry settings.

=======================================================
Bachelor of Science in Tourism Management (BSTM)   PROGRAM LEARNING OUTCOMES (PLOs)
=======================================================
Graduates of the BS in Tourism Management program will be able to:

1. Communicate business concepts and strategies effectively in English, Filipino, and relevant foreign languages.
2. Execute modern planning, product development, and service strategies in multidisciplinary and multicultural teams.
3. Demonstrate professionalism, corporate citizenship, and inclusivity in tourism contexts.
4. Display effective managerial and interpersonal skills with all tourism stakeholders.
5. Apply Christian values in personal and professional contexts aligned with the CICM mission.
6. Incorporate Filipino traits and ethical responsibilities in management decisions and practices.
7. Develop and enhance personality and professional competence in line with local and global tourism trends.

=======================================================
Bachelor of Science in Tourism Management (BSTM)   ADMISSION POLICIES
=======================================================

UNDERGRADUATE FRESHMEN:
- Must pass the SLU College Entrance Examination (SLU-CEE).
- SLU-CEE is held on weekends from mid-October to mid-December.
- First semester admission begins mid-April.

TRANSFER STUDENTS:
- SLU admits transferees for all courses (except BS Medical Laboratory Science).
- Requirements: Qualifying Exam (QE), Personality Test, and Interview.
- Foreign transferees must take the English Proficiency Test (EPT).

GRADUATE PROGRAM STUDENTS:
- Master's: Must hold a Baccalaureate degree.
- Doctorate: Must hold a Master's degree in a related field.

FOREIGN STUDENTS:
- Apply at least 6 months before the academic term.
- Must arrive in Baguio at least 4 weeks before classes.
- Must take: EPT, SLU-CEE / QE / GPEE, and Personality Test.
- Must complete Preadmission Processing at the Student Affairs Office.
- A valid Student Visa is required.
