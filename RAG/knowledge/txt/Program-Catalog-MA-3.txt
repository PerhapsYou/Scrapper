PROGRAM EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES (PEO)  
Three to five years after graduation, graduates of the BS Management Accounting Program are expected to:  
1. Demonstrate professional values, ethics, and attitudes of a management accountant.  
2. Demonstrate in-depth knowledge and skills in management accounting and related disciplines.  
3. Make positive contributions to the profession and support various stakeholders.  
4. Apply creative skills and critical thinking to assist management functions.  

------------------------------------------------------------
PROGRAM LEARNING OUTCOMES (PLO)  
At the end of the program, BSMA graduates should be able to:  
1. Apply competencies in social sciences, mathematics, business laws & taxation, and management philosophies in the field of management accounting.  
2. Communicate business concepts and strategies effectively in English and Filipino using appropriate technology.  
3. Resolve business issues and problems with global and local perspectives using knowledge and technical proficiency.  
4. Employ technology as a tool to capture financial and non-financial information, generate reports, and support decision-making.  
5. Present financial and other reports, analyses, and decisions to management and stakeholders.  
6. Recommend solutions to social and business issues using accounting, financial reporting, taxation, and IT knowledge.  
7. Apply accounting and tax policies in accordance with financial reporting standards and regulations.  
8. Conduct ethical research in management accounting to identify theory-practice gaps and generate knowledge.  
9. Conduct business feasibility studies covering market, finance, HR, and accounting viability.  
10. Apply knowledge and skills to respond successfully to various assessments.  
11. Practice professionalism, corporate citizenship, ethics, and inclusivity in the profession.  
12. Continually develop competencies as a Filipino Louisian management accountant in light of global developments.  
13. Work effectively and independently in multidisciplinary and multicultural teams.  
14. Practice Christian values in personal and professional endeavors as Louisians in the service of the CICM mission.
