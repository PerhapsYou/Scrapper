SLU VISION-MISSION  
Saint Louis University is envisioned as an excellent missionary and transformative educational institution zealous in developing human resources imbued with Christian Spirit and who are creative, competent, and socially involved.

SCHOOL VISION-MISSION  
The School of Teacher Education and Liberal Arts (STELA) is envisioned as an institution for excellence in Teacher and Liberal Arts Education, committed to the CICM mission of education of “missio et excellentia” in the formation and training of competent, creative, socially-involved professionals imbued with Christian Spirit.

PROGRAM EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES (PEO)  
Graduates of the BA Political Science program are expected to:  
- Practice professionalism, social responsibility, and ethical leadership in education, research, government, foreign service, policy analysis, social development, public information, and legal fields.  
- Conduct original research addressing political and policy problems, formulating policy options based on local and global political behavior, ideas, and structures.  
- Embody the distinctiveness of political science, understanding its fundamentals, subfields, theories, and methodologies.  
- Engage as civically responsible members of society through analytical understanding of historical and contemporary political developments.  
- Exhibit critical thinking regarding major political theories and concepts in their pursuit of political, legal, educational, or public service careers.  
- Appraise the unique characteristics and sub-domains of political science.  
- Design research inquiries using appropriate methods informed by political theories and social research frameworks.  
- Communicate sound political arguments and ideas through oral, written, and visual formats in English and Filipino.  
- Respond to pressing national and global political issues with contextual awareness and policy-oriented thinking.  
- Work effectively and independently in interdisciplinary and multicultural teams toward the common good.  
- Uphold professional, social, and ethical standards while demonstrating leadership with Christian values in relevant fields.  
- Practice Christian values in both personal and professional endeavors as Louisians in service of the CICM mission.
