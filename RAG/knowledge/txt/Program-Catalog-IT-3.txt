PROGRAM LEARNING OUTCOMES (PLO)  
Graduates of the BS Information Technology program are expected to:  
1. Manifest understanding of computing, mathematical, and scientific concepts related to the IT discipline.  
2. Use the appropriate tools, technologies, and best practices in addressing the IT-related needs of an organization or community.  
3. Assess technical and user requirements in the analysis of computing problems and provide IT solutions suitable to the problem domain.  
4. Implement IT solutions using current standards, tools, and technologies, while considering user needs, technical environment, and best practices.  
5. Communicate effectively across a wide range of contexts and media.  
6. Function effectively as an accountable individual and as a team player in multicultural and multidisciplinary environments.  
7. Act in accordance with ethical, legal, and social responsibilities as an IT professional, recognizing the local and global impacts of technology.  
8. Adapt to emerging technologies for professional growth.  
9. Practice Christian, Louisian, and Filipino values in both personal and professional life, and actively serve the community.

ADMISSION POLICIES  

**Undergraduate Freshman Students:**  
- Must pass the SLU College Entrance Examination (SLU-CEE)  
- Exams: Mid-October to Mid-December  
- Admission begins: Mid-April  

**Transfer Students:**  
- Accepted into all programs except Bachelor in Medical Laboratory Science  
- Must pass the Qualifying Exam (QE), Personality Test, and Interview  
- Foreign transfers must pass the English Proficiency Test (EPT)  

**Graduate Program Students:**  
- For Master‚Äôs: Must hold a Baccalaureate degree  
- For Doctorate: Must hold a Master‚Äôs degree in a related field  

**Foreign Students:**  
- Apply at least 6 months before the start of term  
- Must arrive in Baguio at least 4 weeks before start of classes  
- Must take and pass: EPT, SLU-CEE/QE/GPEE, and Personality Test  
- Must secure a valid Student Visa  
- Undergo preadmission processing at the Student Affairs Office  

SLU welcomes both local and foreign students, subject to admission policies, academic standards, and Philippine laws.
