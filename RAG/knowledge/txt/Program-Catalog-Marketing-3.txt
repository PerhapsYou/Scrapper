ADMISSION POLICIES  

**Undergraduate Freshman Students:**  
- Must pass SLU College Entrance Examination (SLU-CEE)  
- Conducted mid-October to mid-December  
- Admission starts mid-April  

**Transfer Students:**  
- Accepted in all programs except BS in Medical Laboratory Science  
- Must take Qualifying Exam (QE), then Personality Test and Interview  
- Foreign transferees must pass the English Proficiency Test (EPT)  

**Graduate Program Students:**  
- For Master‚Äôs: must hold a baccalaureate degree  
- For Doctorate: must hold a master‚Äôs degree in a related field  

**Foreign Students:**  
- Must apply at least 6 months before the start of term  
- Must be in Baguio 4 weeks before start of classes  
- Must pass: EPT, SLU-CEE/QE/GPEE, and Personality Test  
- Must undergo Preadmission Processing at the Student Affairs Office
