SAMCIS HISTORY
===========================================================
The School of Accountancy, Management, Computing and Information Studies (SAMCIS) is a merger of:
- The School of Accountancy and Business Management (formerly College of Commerce, one of the original schools of Saint Louis College), and
- The School of Computing and Information Sciences (merged in 2018).

Currently located at the Maryheights Campus of SLU.

===========================================================
VISION & MISSION
===========================================================
The School of Accountancy, Management, Computing and Information Studies (SAMCIS) is envisioned as a center of excellence in Business, Management, and Information Technology Education.

It is committed to:
- The creative, competent, and Christian formation of individuals,
- Producing agents of progress in local and international business and IT environments.

===========================================================
GOALS AND OBJECTIVES
===========================================================
1. Develop business and ICT professionals grounded in Christian values, acting as responsible, ethical professionals and global citizens.
2. Innovate academic programs that are responsive to industry and community needs.
3. Equip faculty and students with globally competitive knowledge and skills in management and computing.
4. Generate relevant management and ICT research.
5. Provide continuing education, expert advice, and community engagement in management and ICT.
6. Sustain long-term growth through creative and self-reliant strategies.
